Mary House Catholic Worker of Austin, founded in 1991, is a small Catholic Worker community which offers housing to homeless adults with critical and/or terminal illnesses who have no other resources. Contact: Lynn Goodman-Strauss, Mike Flynn.

Consider contacting the Mary House Catholic Worker to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
