Catholic Worker on Wheels
124 Wadsworth St
Syracuse NY 13203

Phone: (315) 479-9994

Website: cwonwheels.com

Email: cwonwheels@outlook.com

Daily hot breakfasts delivered to friends on the street and in homeless encampments in Syracuse. Full-course breakfasts ordered from a menu delivered on Saturdays to friends and families with children. Clothing, household needs, transportation assistance and limited hospitality in our house also provided.

Consider contacting the Catholic Worker on Wheels to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
