Poverello House of Hospitality, a Catholic Worker Community, is inspired by the Franciscan Charisma and Benedictine Hospitality. We provide food, clothing, shelter and spiritually-centered care to our resident guests and an outreach to our needy brothers and sisters of the community.

We share a firm belief in the God-given dignity of every human person; and we are committed to nonviolence, voluntary poverty, and the Works of Mercy as a way of life.

Consider contacting the Eugene Catholic Worker Community to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
