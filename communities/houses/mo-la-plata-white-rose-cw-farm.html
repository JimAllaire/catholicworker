Human and horse-powered land-based living; hospitality to homeless; group immersion trips; building a cooperative village with our neighbors.

Volunteers needed:
We offer live-in short or long-term volunteer opportunities. Come help with candle making, hospitality, tomato canning, wild edible foraging, draft horse farming, natural building, permaculture design...and much more! We also invite schools, churches, nonprofits, etc. to bring groups for day or week long immersion, service, and educational opportunities. We are 6 miles from an Amtrak stop! If you are interested, contact us by mail or phone.

Consider contacting the White Rose Catholic Worker Farm to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
