Website: Dorothy Day Spiritual Center

The Dorothy Day Spiritual Center began in May 2016. It is the very first Catholic Worker Movement in South Korea. The Center runs the website which provides news and essays related to the Catholic Worker Movement and Christian spirituality. It is the collaborative work of people from diverse Christian traditions who are affiliated with the Catholic Worker Movement. There are weekly/monthly seminars and training sessions on social justice and spirituality. The Center also publishes and prints a bi-monthly newspaper. It is distributed free of charge to all who desire to receive. The Dorothy Day Spiritual Center is maintained by Han Sang-Bong, a Catholic lay theologian and author of several books on spirituality. For more information or to contact Sang-Bong with comments or suggestions use the above email address.

Consider contacting the Dorothy Day Spiritual Center to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
