Saint Isidore Catholic Worker Farm
2752 Clay Hollow Rd
Cuba City WI 53807

Email: CatholicWorkerSchool@gmail.com

Phone: 608.568.3630

Intentional Christian community on 25 acres in the southwest corner of Wisconsin near borders of Illinois and Iowa. Organic gardens; cows, chickens, and bees. Subsistence living, gift economy, and small CSA. Shared morning prayer; activism; hospitality to pilgrims and the marginalized. Agronomic University education workshops. Communal singing; barn dances.

Consider contacting the Saint Isidore Catholic Worker Farm to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
