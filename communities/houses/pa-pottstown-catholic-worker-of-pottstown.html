The Catholic Worker of Pottstown is a small community that focuses on daily prayer, hosting Round Table Discussions, and the developmental of a small vegetable farm for the purposes of developing homesteading skills, to cultivating the land, as well as assisting others to use whatever land or space they have to grow their own food.

Food grown by CWP will be available to local food banks or families in need through our spring/summer "FREE-SA."

For info on the next Round Table, opportunities to learn or volunteer at the homestead, or more info on the "FREE-SA" please don't hesitate to contact us.

Consider contacting the Catholic Worker of Pottstown to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
