Su Casa Catholic Worker Su Casa Catholic Worker is a community of hospitality and healing in the Back of the Yards. We hold space for families in crisis, Frieda's Place Soup Kitchen, a large community garden, and The LetUsBreathe Collective, a black-led abolitionist alliance of artists and activists.

Consider contacting the Su Casa Catholic Worker Community to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
