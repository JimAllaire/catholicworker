Banyan Abbey
1105 NE 31 Terrace, Homestead, FL 33033

Email: banyanabbey@gmail.com

Website: Banyan Abbey

A hermitage under the episcopal and spiritual care of the Apostolic Episcopal Church. We provide education, spiritual direction, psychotherapy, a wedding ministry and works of mercy and empowerment, to the community for the purpose of glorifying Almighty God and encouraging the spiritual evolution of all souls in the Catholic Worker tradition. We teach Personalism and advocate the inalienable human & civil rights of all people as endowed by our Creator.

Consider contacting Banyan Abbey to volunteer your time or to participate in their work.
