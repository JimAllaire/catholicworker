Email: pastordanielmedina@gmail.com

Website: Banyan Abbey

Banyan Abbey is a Miami-based ministry in the Catholic Worker tradition and the American Transcendentalist movement. We provide counseling, community organization, mediation, nonviolence training, and interfaith partnerships with local justice non-profits. The South Florida Meditation and Mindfulness Society was born out of Banyan Abbey as a place to offer resources for healing, empowerment, and tools for spiritual evolution. Meditation and Yoga courses are available several times over the course of the year. Rev Dr Daniel Medina is the Founder and Abbot of Banyan Abbey. We identify as Anglican/Old Catholic, Benedictine, and Universalist. We are located in the historic Little Havana neighborhood. Please contact us at pastordanielmedina@gmail.com.

Consider contacting Banyan Abbey to volunteer your time or to participate in their work.
