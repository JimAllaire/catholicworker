All are invited to the DDCWHCC - 210 S. Carrizo Street - every third Sunday of the month to share breakfast with our homeless brothers and sisters and neighbors in need. Come early and help with prep at 6:30 a.m. Breakfast is served from 7:45 a.m. to 9 a.m., followed by clean-up. Donations of bananas, pastries and juice always welcome! Personal hygiene items for men and women are always needed. Please come and be a sign of hope to our brothers and sisters who live on the streets.

Consider contacting the name Dorothy Day Catholic Worker House to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
