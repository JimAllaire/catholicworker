Dorothy Day House of Hospitality
126 Juliette St
Greenslopes, Brisbane, Australia

Email: cw.brisbane@gmail.com

Website: Dorothy Day House of Hospitality

Publication: Love Against the Machine

A community of young adults living in inner city Brisbane, Australia, who engage with Brisbane's marginalized by providing hospitality, a place to belong and friendships. We actively seek to connect with the wider community through activism; running craft, poetry and documentary nights; morning prayers; Food Not Bombs; involvement with the Waiter's Union; and animal sanctuary (currently housing chickens, cute cats, Guinea pigs and a dog).

Consider contacting the Dorothy Day House of Hospitality to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
