Francis House Catholic Worker formed in March of 2019. We do not own property and we rent from a local church 5 rooms where we serve food every Sunday (Holy Posole) and 200 burritos. We also go out into the streets to share the burritos and have since October of 2017. We have a meditation room and we teach centering prayer/meditation in English and Spanish. We have a women's support group meeting weekly as well as a mixed spirituality group going on now for 3 years. We do bimonthly retreats in local retreat houses. Our other main ministry is with the Asylum Seekers in El Paso. We gather donations of food, clothes and toiletries and take them to Annunciation House every Friday.

Consider contacting the Francis House Catholic Worker to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
