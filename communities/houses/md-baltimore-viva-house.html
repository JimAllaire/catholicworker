Publication: Enthusiasm

Viva House opened in 1968 and for the past half a century our community has provided hospitality (soup kitchen, food pantry ) for our neighbors, and we do our best to resist the injustices of capitalism, militarism, racism, and sexism. Willa Bickham and Brendan Walsh have published a book, The Long Loneliness In Baltimore: Stories Along the Way, in an attempt to explain our work through essays and art. (Apprentice House Press, Loyola University Maryland, 2019). Copies are also available from Viva House.

Consider contacting the Viva House, Baltimore Catholic Worker to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
