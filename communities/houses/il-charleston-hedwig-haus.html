Hedwig Haus of Hospitality
227 Jackson Ave
Charleston IL 61920-1948

Website: Hedwig Haus

Catholic Worker Community in Charleston Il provides shelter for women and children. The community currently has three houses: St. Hedwig Haus, Augustus Tolton House, and Dorothy Day House. All three serve women and children and provide safe haven. We are always in need of resident Catholic Workers. If you are interested, please call 217-345-6901 or email Roy Lanham at rlanham@eiunewman.org.

Consider contacting the Hedwig Haus of Hospitality to volunteer your time or to participate in their community life.
