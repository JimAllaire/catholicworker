Day House
2640 Trumbull Ave
Detroit MI 48216

Phone: (313) 974-7752

Email: dayhousedetroit@gmail.com

Facebook: Day House Detroit Facebook Page

Publication: On the Edge

Day House was founded in 1978. For over forty years, the 10-bedroom house has served as a transitional home for folks (mostly women) experiencing housing instability. We also provide general services like showers, laundry, mail, meeting space for activist organizations, and a weekly community mass and dinner. Day House is closely connected with Manna Meal soup kitchen, which serves out St. Peter's Episcopal Church just a few blocks away. We welcome visitors and potential volunteers! Please contact us at dayhousedetroit@gmail.com.
